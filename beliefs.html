Statement of Faith

Historic Baptist Doctrine, Clearly Stated

At Zion Hill Baptist Church, we hold to the historic Baptist faith. Our doctrine is centered on the pillars below, each grounded in Scripture.

What We Believe

Scripture links open in a new tab (KJV) for easy reading and study.

The Holy Scriptures

Scripture Reference: 2 Timothy 3:16

We believe the Holy Bible was written by men divinely inspired and is a perfect treasure of heavenly instruction. We exclusively use the King James Version (KJV) in all our services and teaching, believing it to be the preserved Word of God for the English-speaking people.

The True God

Scripture Reference: Matthew 28:19

We believe there is one, and only one, living and true God, an infinite, intelligent Spirit, the Maker and Supreme Ruler of heaven and earth. In the unity of the Godhead, there are three persons: the Father, the Son, and the Holy Ghost.

The Fall of Man

Scripture Reference: Romans 5:12

We believe that man was created in holiness, but by voluntary transgression fell from that holy state; in consequence of which all mankind are now sinners by nature and by choice.

The Way of Salvation

Scripture Reference: Ephesians 2:8-9

We believe that the salvation of sinners is wholly of grace through the mediatorial offices of the Son of God, Jesus Christ, who by the appointment of the Father, freely took upon Him our nature, yet without sin, and by His death made a full vicarious atonement for our sins.

Justification

Scripture Reference: Romans 5:1

We believe that the great Gospel blessing which Christ secures to such as believe in Him is Justification; that Justification includes the pardon of sin and the promise of eternal life on principles of righteousness.

The Local Church

Scripture Reference: Acts 2:41-42

We believe that a visible church of Christ is a congregation of baptized believers, associated by covenant in the faith and fellowship of the Gospel. We operate as an independent body, self-governing under the lordship of Jesus Christ.
