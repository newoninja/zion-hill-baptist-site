What We Believe

Each doctrine below includes a Scripture reference link for study in the King James Bible.

The Holy Scriptures

We believe the Holy Bible was written by men divinely inspired and is a perfect treasure of heavenly instruction. We use the King James Version in all services and teaching, believing it to be God's preserved Word for English-speaking people.

Scripture: 2 Timothy 3:16 (KJV)

The True God

We believe there is one, and only one, living and true God. In the unity of the Godhead there are three persons: the Father, the Son, and the Holy Ghost.

Scripture: Matthew 28:19 (KJV)

The Fall of Man

We believe man was created in holiness, but by voluntary transgression fell from that state; therefore all mankind are now sinners by nature and by choice.

Scripture: Romans 5:12 (KJV)

The Way of Salvation

We believe salvation of sinners is wholly of grace through Jesus Christ, who took upon Him our nature and by His death made full atonement for our sins.

Scripture: Ephesians 2:8-9 (KJV)

Justification

We believe justification includes the pardon of sin and promise of eternal life to all who believe in Jesus Christ.

Scripture: Romans 5:1 (KJV)

The Local Church

We believe a visible church is a congregation of baptized believers associated in covenant faith and Gospel fellowship. We operate as an independent body under Christ's lordship.

Scripture: Acts 2:41-42 (KJV)
