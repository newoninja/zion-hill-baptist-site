First-Time Guest FAQ

A few helpful details before your first visit.

What should I wear?

You will see a range of Sunday attire. We value reverence in worship and warmly welcome every guest.

Is there parking close to the building?

Yes. On-site parking is available with convenient access near the church entrance.

Are children welcome in services?

Absolutely. Families are welcome in Sunday School and worship services as we learn and grow together.

Can I listen before I attend?

Yes. You can hear the Sunday radio broadcast at 8:05 AM on WHKP AM 1450 / 107.7 FM and browse the sermon archive online.

Need Help Before Sunday?

Call us or send a message and we will help you plan your visit with confidence.
