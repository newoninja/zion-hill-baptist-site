Our Story

A Heritage of Faith in the Blue Ridge Mountains

Zion Hill Baptist Church began with a tent revival and remains a local lighthouse in Zirconia, North Carolina.

Home / Our History

A Century of Faithful Witness

In the spring of 1914, in the quiet community then known as Splendor, North Carolina, a small group of believers gathered under a canvas tent to worship. They had no building, but they had a deep hunger for the Word of God and a vision for a local lighthouse in the mountains.

On May 24, 1916, that vision took permanent shape as Zion Hill Baptist Church moved into its first building. Through changing times, the message preached within these walls has stayed the same.

Today, Zion Hill remains a traditional, independent Baptist church dedicated to old-time worship, Christian fellowship, and the authority of the King James Bible.
