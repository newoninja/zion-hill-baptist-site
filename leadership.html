Meet Our Pastor

Pastor Blair Stewart & Tina Stewart

Zion Hill is shepherded by a pastor and family who love this community and are committed to faithful, old-time Bible preaching.

A Warm Welcome

Pastor Blair Stewart and his wife, Tina, have a heart for families in Henderson County and the surrounding mountain communities. Their ministry is centered on clear KJV preaching, compassionate pastoral care, and strengthening the local church through prayer, discipleship, and fellowship.

Whether you are searching for a church home, returning to church, or simply visiting the area, Pastor Blair and Tina desire for you to feel welcomed from your first Sunday.

Questions Before You Visit?

Call us at (828) 692-4544. We are happy to help with service information, directions, or prayer requests.

Ministry Focus

Biblical Preaching
